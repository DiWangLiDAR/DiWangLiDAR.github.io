Overview

I am currently an associate professor at Department of Remote Sensing Science and Technology in Xidian University. I received my Ph.D. degree in Photogrammetry and Remote Sensing from Vienna University of Technology in Austria in 2018 and was a Postdoctoral Researcher at Aalto University in Finland from September 2018 to August 2020. My primary research lies in LiDAR remote sensing, point cloud intelligent processing and its applications on smart city, ecological monitoring, and precision forestry/agriculture.
